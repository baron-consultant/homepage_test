    Baron Consultants - Conclusion of a business agreement with the Future Civil Engineering Department of Kangwon National University for cooperation in education and research on advanced civil engineering
    2026.05.19
    Baron Consultants and Kangwon National University's Future Civil Engineering Department have signed an industry-academic cooperation agreement for joint cooperation in high-tech fields such as smart construction, BIM, and digital twins and to cultivate professional manpower.
    The two organizations agreed to establish an industry-academic cooperation system in structural analysis, safety diagnosis, eco-friendly and carbon neutral infrastructure, and to strengthen working-level capabilities and promote technology exchanges through joint research and field-oriented education programs.

    Source: Kangwon National University
