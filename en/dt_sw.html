      Engineering Software

        Software for
        Digital Transformation

        The need for software is increasing in the infrastructure construction industry, which aims to transition into a high value-added industry through Digital Transformation, including the creative improvement of work processes and the comprehensive adoption of BIM design.

        The adoption required is not general-purpose software commonly applicable to a wide range of fields, but specialized engineering software tailored to the infrastructure construction industry.

          Need for
          Engineering Software

          Expert Software
          refers to software that produces and generates project deliverables by applying specialized technology in specific fields such as civil engineering and construction.

          In the rapidly changing construction industry, dependence on foreign software can lead to technological subjugation, increased cost burden, weakened competitiveness, and existential crisis for companies; thus, the development of domestic Expert Software to overcome this is necessary.

            Direction of
            Engineering Software

            We must transition to a new paradigm that overcomes the limitations of existing foreign software, enhances technological innovation and efficiency, and integrates civil engineering expertise with IT-based digital technology to achieve genuine Digital Transformation in the infrastructure construction industry.
