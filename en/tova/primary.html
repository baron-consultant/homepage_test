Zone Subdivision and Aggregation
The subdivision function splits a single zone into multiple zones. Users can define the proportion and location of subdivided zones, and the OD table is automatically generated based on the subdivision ratios. The aggregation function combines multiple zones into a single zone, and the OD flows between the merged zones are treated as internal trips.
