    Baron consultants Officially Launches Homegrown Solution "EG-BIM"
    2026.01.21
    [Korea Economy = Reporter Ahn Jae-min] baron consultants announced on the 20th that it will officially launch civil engineering specialized programs including its own 2D and 3D integrated design solution 'EG-BIM'. 'EG-BIM' is a construction-specific CAD that integrates 2D design and 3D viewer function. Prior to the official launch, the stability of the project was verified through commercialization tests in Saman Corporation and Hanmaek Technology, and its introduction is spreading to a number of designers such as Shinmyung ENC and Seojeong Engineering. The company plans to further strengthen its technological competitiveness by adding 3D modeling functions in the second half of this year. In addition, baron consultants unveiled a number of digital innovation solutions. Major lineup includes △IoT-based drilling survey digital innovation tool 'GAIA' △ Korean-style integrated traffic demand analysis solution 'TOVA' △ sewer network design integrated solution 'I-PIPE' etc. In March, 'KNGIL' that automatically generates basic status reports is also set to be released.
