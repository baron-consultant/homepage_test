    BIG Room

    The BIG Room is a space, unlike traditional co-located
    design offices or construction site offices, where Smart
    Construction techniques such as BIM, Lean, and Pull
    Planning are utilized. It serves as a centralized area
    where project stakeholders gather to collaboratively
    review and discuss project-related issues.

    The BIG Room enables seamless inter-cooperation and
    collaboration, facilitating optimized schedule management
    and rapid decision-making.

    BIG Room for the
    Infrastructure Construction

    Infrastructure facilities are planned across vast and linear large-scale areas, requiring high precision for BIM-based construction information.

    Simultaneous collaboration among multiple stakeholders for each trade is essential, and information can be effectively and efficiently delivered and shared through a BIG Room environment utilizing large-format displays (signage).
